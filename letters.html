Letters

from our executive team

Ege Kağan Güncan

Secretary General

Dear Delegates, Organizing Team, and Academic Team Candidates,

It is my great pleasure and honor to extend to you a warm greeting to the First Annual Session of the Çiğli Model United Nations.

As Secretary General, I am delighted to welcome each of you to what promises to be an inspiring and enriching experience. This conference represents not only a platform for diplomacy, collaboration, and critical thinking but also the beginning of a tradition built on excellence, inclusivity, and innovation.

Whether you are applying as a delegate, an academic team member, or part of the organization, your role will be instrumental in shaping the success and spirit of CMUN. I look forward to witnessing your passion, ideas, and leadership as we come together to tackle global challenges through dialogue and diplomacy.

Join us in making history at our inaugural session, where every voice matters and every perspective counts.

Yours sincerely,
Secretary General

Asmin Tumur

Director General

Hello everyone,

My name is Asmin Tumur. As a member of the executive team of the First Official Session of Çiğli Model United Nations, I would like to express how pleased I am to welcome you all in advance. I hope to see everyone at this conference, which will take shape with your valuable participation and support.

Our executive team, composed of students from various schools, shares a common goal: to organize a conference in Çiğli and to make it accessible to a wide student audience.

We aim to create an environment where participants can express themselves freely, share their ideas, and develop their diplomatic skills. It is our greatest desire to host a conference atmosphere where every delegate's opinion is valued, constructive debate is encouraged, and a solution-oriented mindset is embraced.
